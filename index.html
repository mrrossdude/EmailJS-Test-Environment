Welcome to the PicoSmiths Custom Keycap Editor

Use this tool to convert any image to a custom keycap. Make sure to read these tips to get the best quality possible.

Magic Wand Tool

If you're using images with a solid colour background like JPG or BMP, use the Magic Wand to strip away backgrounds. Simply click the area you want to remove and hit apply.

Image Adjustment Tool

If your image is more complex or the Magic Wand isn't working for you, try enabling the Adjustment Tool and use the sliders to fine tune your image. This tool automatically strips out white backgrounds, so you might want to click the INVERT button if your image is on a dark background.
